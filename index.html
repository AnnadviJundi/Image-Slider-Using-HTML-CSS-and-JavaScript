Attack On Titan
"Attack on Titan" is a Japanese anime and manga series where humanity fights for survival against giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. Eren Yeager and his friends join the military to combat the Titan threat, unraveling mysteries about the Titans' origins and facing political intrigue. The series explores themes of freedom, sacrifice, and the consequences of discovering the truth about their world.
Sword Art Online
"Sword Art Online" is a Japanese anime set in a near-future where players, including protagonist Kirito, become trapped in a virtual reality MMORPG. To escape, they must reach the final level, facing perilous challenges and the threat of real-world death if they fail in the game.
My Hero Academia
"My Hero Academia" is an anime and manga series set in a world of superpowers called "Quirks." It follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who receives a powerful Quirk from the legendary hero All Might. Midoriya attends U.A. High School to train and become a hero, facing challenges and villains along the way.
Naruto
"Naruto" follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja aiming to become the strongest and earn the title of Hokage. Endowed with a powerful fox spirit, he overcomes challenges, forms bonds with friends like Sasuke and Sakura, and matures into a skilled ninja on a quest for peace in the ninja world.
Haikyuu
"Haikyuu!!" follows Shoyo Hinata's journey as a determined high school volleyball player aiming for national success despite his shorter stature. The series showcases the growth, challenges, and camaraderie of the Karasuno High School volleyball team in their pursuit of greatness on the court.
Eighty Six
"Eighty Six" is a Japanese light novel series set in a dystopian future where the Republic of San Magnolia uses a marginalized group known as the "Eighty-Six" to pilot autonomous drones in their war against the Legion. The series explores themes of discrimination, war, and the moral complexities of dehumanizing a group of people for the sake of survival.
Tenki No Ko
"Tenki no Ko" (Weathering with You) is an animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. It follows Hodaka, a runaway, who befriends Hina, a girl with the ability to control the weather. The story explores themes of love and sacrifice against the backdrop of beautifully animated weather phenomena.
